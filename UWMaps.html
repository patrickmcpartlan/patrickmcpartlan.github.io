I'm currently getting my Master's Degree at UW-Madison in GIS and Web Map Development. This page shows some projects I've worked on during my Graphic Design in Cartography Course. Stay tuned! Many more to come!

Lab 1: Longform Infographic

This map is a longform infographic on the impacts of COVID 19 on the Agricultural Food Supply Chain. The point was to incorporate a bivariate map, along with design other components using adobe illustrator.

Lab 2: Bike Tour Map

This labs focus was to create a new bike tour using various criteria. The bike route needed to be 18 days cover 100 miles a day with a wide variety of terain. The map is supposed to also link up the land cover map and the elevation rasters to form a nice base layer. This route goes from Charlotte, NC to St. Louis to Little Rock to New Orleans. It has plenty of scenic views along the way. Please reach out to me if you'd like to recieve the shapefile or KML file of the route!

Lab 3: Movie Themed MapBox Tile Set

This online tile map is a dedication to the life work of Rachel Carson, specifically her book Silent Spring. The tile sets are derived off of the color scheme of images found on the online publication of her book found on the new yorker. She was a pioneer in order to get the harmful pesticide DDT banned from sale in the United Staes. I'd watch that movie twice and I hope the map gives people a better perspective on just how bad the pollinators have it.
